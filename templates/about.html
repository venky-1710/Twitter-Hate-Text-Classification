About the Project

Our Mission

The Twitter Hate Speech Detector aims to promote a safer digital environment by identifying harmful content and raising awareness about hate speech online.

How It Works

Our application uses machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of tweets to detect patterns associated with hate speech. The model uses the following techniques:

  • Natural Language Processing to understand text context
  • TF-IDF vectorization to weigh important words
  • Logistic Regression with balanced class weighting
  • Identification of aggressive language and hate speech markers
