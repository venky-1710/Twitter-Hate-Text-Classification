Help & FAQ

How to Use

1

Enter Text

Type or paste the text you want to analyze in the input box on the home page.

2

Click Analyze

Press the "Analyze" button or use Ctrl+Enter to submit your text for analysis.

3

Review Results

Check the analysis result, which includes a hate speech probability score, identified aggressive words, and severity rating.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the detection model?

Our model achieves approximately 92% accuracy on test data. However, context and nuance can be challenging, so we recommend using the tool as an aid rather than a definitive authority.

What happens to the text I submit?

Your text is processed only to provide analysis and is not stored on our servers. We prioritize user privacy and data security.

Can I report false positives or negatives?

Yes, we welcome feedback to improve our model. Please use the "Report" feature that appears below the analysis results to submit your feedback.

What languages are supported?

Currently, our model only supports English text. We're working on expanding to other languages in future updates.
